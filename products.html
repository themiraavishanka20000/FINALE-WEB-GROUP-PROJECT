Seed Products

The state sector, particularly the Department of Agriculture's Seed and Planting Material Development Centre, dominates seed production in Sri Lanka, which is a biodiversity hotspot. The private sector imports the majority of the country's high-quality seed and distributes it to farmers. Because Sri Lanka is heavily reliant on imported seed, the Department of Agriculture and Bangladesh's Lal Teer Seed launched an initiative in 2017 to expand vegetable seed production in the nation. The project will be executed on the grounds of Mahailluppallama's Field Crops Research and Development Institute.
