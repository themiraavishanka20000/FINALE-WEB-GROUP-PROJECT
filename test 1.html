Plastic Recycle




Here we do the process of taking plastic and reprocessing them into useful and functional products. Our goal for doing this is to reduce the high rates of plastic pollution. Since plastic is durable, inexpensive, and lightweight the recycling process is easy to be done.

Beach Cleaning



We help in keeping our beach clean by removing solid matter, chemicals and organic substances found. This process helps in public health as well as the ecology and tourism, also helps to maintain the sustainability. The method we use here for beach cleaning is the basic method of using adequate human resources and funds.

Lecturing




To acknowledge the people, we are organizing awareness programs. These programs are hosted by well-educated expertise. In these programs, we are explaining about disadvantages of cutting trees and polluting the environment. Thereby the people will get the understanding and helps in the betterment of life.

Protect Animales




This is a Sri Lankan animal rescue and welfare organization with the collaboration of Embark organization. We are conducting vaccinations, education programmers and campaigns for the benefits of homeless dogs.
